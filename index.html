GEO Workbench Lab

Check your page for AI search.

GEO Workbench Lab is a local project for testing how a web page might appear in AI search and answer systems. It helps you look at a page, write prompts, and save notes from testing.

Run a GEO test

What it does

Checks a web page

Use it to review a page and see if the main content is easy to inspect.

Helps create prompts

Write simple questions that you can test in AI search and answer tools.

Saves testing notes

Keep a basic record of what you tested and what you found.

Why use it

If you are trying to understand whether your landing page is clear for AI tools, GEO Workbench Lab gives you a simple place to start.
